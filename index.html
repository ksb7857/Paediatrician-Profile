Specialist Paediatrician

North Shore Private Hospital

Dr Ketaki Sharma (Ket) is a compassionate and experienced paediatrician who prioritises listening to families and delivering tailored, family-centred support.

Ket provides consultations for:

  • Newborn and infant health concerns;
  • Reflux;
  • Growth or weight concerns;
  • Suspected cow’s milk protein intolerance;
  • Food allergies (skin prick testing available on-site, if clinically required);
  • Eczema;
  • Hayfever;
  • Constipation, and other common childhood concerns.

Professional Background

Ket studied medicine at the University of New South Wales, then undertook specialist paediatric training through the Sydney Children’s Hospital Network to become a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians (FRACP). Her commitment to evidence-based practice and lifelong learning ensures she stays informed of changes in clinical guidelines. She is an Associate Member of the Australasian Society for Clinical Immunology and Allergy (ASCIA) and a member of the Australian Paediatric Society. Beyond her professional training, Ket has two children, one of whom has multiple food allergies, eczema, and hayfever. This lived experience informs her empathetic, practical approach to supporting families facing similar challenges.

Ket is also a Staff Specialist Paediatrician at:

  • The Children’s Hospital at Westmead (Specialist Immunisation Service)
  • The National Centre for Immunisation Research and Surveillance (NCIRS)
She has a Masters of Public Health and is passionate about promoting the evidence-based use of vaccines in Australia and globally. Her work includes:
  • Developing national immunisation guidelines;
  • Contributing to research (view her research profile); and
  • Contributing to global health initiatives, including collaborations with the World Health Organization.

Teaching

Ket enjoys teaching and holds a Conjoint Appointment as Clinical Lecturer in the Discipline of Child and Adolescent Health at the University of Sydney. She also regularly delivers educational updates for GPs and other specialists.
