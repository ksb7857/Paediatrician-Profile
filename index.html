Specialist Paediatrician – North Shore Private Hospital

Dr Ketaki Sharma (Ket) is a compassionate and experienced paediatrician who prioritises listening to families and delivering tailored, family-centred support.

Ket provides consultations for:

  • Newborn and infant health concerns
  • Reflux
  • Concerns about growth or weight
  • Suspected cow’s milk protein intolerance
  • Food allergies (skin prick testing available on-site, if clinically required)
  • Eczema
  • Hayfever
  • Constipation, and other common childhood concerns
